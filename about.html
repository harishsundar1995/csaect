Our Founder

SYED AFZAL

SYED AFZAL


Bangalore 560046, Contact: +91 9886128677

Managing Director: Crescent Affa Enterprises

Founder: Crescent Sam Affa Educational and charitable trust

Year of Birth: 1961

At the age of 12, Mr. Syed Afzal started supporting his elder brother Haji Syed Ismail in a small Bakery. In the year 1979, Alongside bakery, he started home delivering bottled milk under the umbrella of his brother-in-law Abdul Rehman in a milk agency.

In 1981 seeing the hardship of the young and dynamic boy, Mr. K.Peeran, father of P Abdul Rehman suggested and enrolled him to pursue his career in Electrical works. Mr. Syed Afzal started his studies at the Industrial Training Institute in Hosur Road near Bangalore Dairy Circle. He successfully completed the Electrical Wireman certification in the year 1984. His course notes with beautiful sketches are an inspiration to the younger generation in the family.

With a lot of hard work and multitasking in different portfolios, Mr.Syed Afzal practiced and focused on his electrical career. He came up with a lot of new recipes for the bakery like the Crescent special badam salt milk biscuit which was great hit among the customers those days.

In 1989, Mr.Syed Afzal started his dream career in Electrical works obtaining class 1 Electrical Contractor License from the department of Electrical Inspectorate under the name of M/s Crescent Electricals. He started with arranging temporary basis power supply to function halls & all types of exhibitions, fairs etc. at different places. Some of the events that were a feather in his cap were as follows:

Stona Exhibition at Palace Ground, Bangalore. Various IT Exibitions Niligiri’s Cake Exhibition. Bio Tech & Bangalore Tech Summit Pedicon – National Conference. Christian Piece Function. India Orthopedic Conference. Aero India Exhibition National Consumer Fair, Bangalore. National Level Exhibition. All Party-Political Functions, Palace Ground, Bangalore. Gemini Circus, Bangalore. Electronic & Electrical Trade Exhibition, Bangalore. Fun World Amusement Park, Bangalore. IBM Conference. Time Décor Interior Exhibition. Global Investors Meet.
His other works included internal & external Electrification works, Transformer Yard Fencing work including civil & Painting for Karnataka Electrical Board, KEB Maintenance work of existing feeder pillar boxes, repairing, reconditioning by replacing required material, removing of existing cable and termination of the same, etc.
Mr. Syed AfzaL is known for his far-sighted vision, hard work and commitment. He has set an example to all the younger family members and supported and guided them to walk the tough path. His formula of success is nothing is IMPOSSBLE.

His Skills: Team Player Leadership Experience Adaptability and Accountability Communication Skills Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Entrepreneurship

Giving back to society is his mantra of citizenship. He leaves no stone unturned to help the needy and those in distress. To support his vision of making a difference in society, he started the CRESECENT SAM AFFA EDUCATONAL & CHARITABLE TRUST on the year 2020.

During the covid times, when the whole world was reeling under the lockdown, he kept himself and the family motivated by sowing the seeds of the CSAECT to help those in need.

Not leaving behind any strata of society, he pioneered offering medical assistance during covid times by providing oxygen cylinders to a nearby hospital, supplying cable to a nearby school and food packets worth Rs1.5 lakhs to the Good Aid foundation. The trust supported other smaller trusts in their initiatives like the Milk Mission and many more.

CSAECT works day & night under his founder ship to support all those who need help. The trust supports people by providing regular ration to more than 85 families, medical assistance to the sick, interest-free loans to help people establish their livelihood. He is a person who has great value and belief that the younger generation needs to get educated and he will go all lengths to support the youth to get educated.

His message to society is

“Helping others is helping ourselves”

Yours Sincerely Syed Afzal
