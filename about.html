About the Founder

SYED AFZAL


Chairman and Managing Director: Crescent Affa Enterprises

Bangalore 560046, Contact: +91 9886128677

Founder: Crescent Sam Affa Educational and charitable trust

Year of Birth: 1961

At the age of 12, Mr. Syed Afzal started supporting his elder brother Haji Syed Ismail in a small Bakery. In the year 1979, Alongside bakery, he started home delivering bottled milk under the umbrella of his brother-in-law Abdul Rehman in a milk agency.

In 1981 seeing the hardship of the young and dynamic boy, Mr. K.Peeran, father of P Abdul Rehman suggested and enrolled him to pursue his career in Electrical works. Mr. Syed Afzal started his studies at the Industrial Training Institute in Hosur Road near Bangalore Dairy Circle. He successfully completed the Electrical Wireman certification in the year 1984. His course notes with beautiful sketches are an inspiration to the younger generation in the family.

With a lot of hard work and multitasking in different portfolios, Mr.Syed Afzal practiced and focused on his electrical career. He came up with a lot of new recipes for the bakery like the Crescent special badam salt milk biscuit which was great hit among the customers those days.

In 1989, Mr.Syed Afzal started his dream career in Electrical works obtaining class 1 Electrical Contractor License from the department of Electrical Inspectorate under the name of M/s Crescent Electricals. He started with arranging temporary basis power supply to function halls & all types of exhibitions, fairs etc. at different places. Some of the events that were a feather in his cap were as follows:

Stona Exhibition at Palace Ground, Bangalore. Various IT Exibitions Niligiri’s Cake Exhibition. Bio Tech & Bangalore Tech Summit Pedicon – National Conference. Christian Piece Function. India Orthopedic Conference. Aero India Exhibition National Consumer Fair, Bangalore. National Level Exhibition. All Party-Political Functions, Palace Ground, Bangalore. Gemini Circus, Bangalore. Electronic & Electrical Trade Exhibition, Bangalore. Fun World Amusement Park, Bangalore. IBM Conference. Time Décor Interior Exhibition. Global Investors Meet.

His other works included internal & external Electrification works, Transformer Yard Fencing work including civil & Painting for Karnataka Electrical Board, KEB Maintenance work of existing feeder pillar boxes, repairing, reconditioning by replacing required material, removing of existing cable and termination of the same, etc.

Mr. Syed AfzaL is known for his far-sighted vision, hard work and commitment. He has set an example to all the younger family members and supported and guided them to walk the tough path. His formula of success is nothing is IMPOSSBLE.

His Skills: Team Player Leadership Experience Adaptability and Accountability Communication Skills Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Entrepreneurship

Giving back to society is his mantra of citizenship. He leaves no stone unturned to help the needy and those in distress. To support his vision of making a difference in society, he started the CRESECENT SAM AFFA EDUCATONAL & CHARITABLE TRUST on the year 2020.

During the covid times, when the whole world was reeling under the lockdown, he kept himself and the family motivated by sowing the seeds of the CSAECT to help those in need.

Not leaving behind any strata of society, he pioneered offering medical assistance during covid times by providing oxygen cylinders to a nearby hospital, supplying cable to a nearby school and food packets worth Rs1.5 lakhs to the Good Aid foundation. The trust supported other smaller trusts in their initiatives like the Milk Mission and many more.

CSAECT works day & night under his founder ship to support all those who need help. The trust supports people by providing regular ration to more than 85 families, medical assistance to the sick, interest-free loans to help people establish their livelihood. He is a person who has great value and belief that the younger generation needs to get educated and he will go all lengths to support the youth to get educated.

His message to society is :

“Helping others is helping ourselves”

Yours Sincerely Syed Afzal

Educational Funds

We are dedicated to assisting underprivileged students who require financial support to pursue their education through our regular scholarship programs. Our primary goal is to empower these students by providing them with opportunities that may have otherwise been out of reach. By offering scholarships, we aim to alleviate the financial burden they face and enable them to focus on their studies and future aspirations.

Through our scholarship programs, we strive to bridge the gap between their aspirations and limited resources. We firmly believe that every individual, regardless of their socio-economic background, deserves an equal chance to excel academically and build a promising future.

Our commitment extends beyond the provision of financial aid. We work closely with educational institutions, mentors, and volunteers to provide a comprehensive support system for our scholarship recipients. We offer guidance, mentorship, and resources to ensure that they have the necessary tools to succeed not only academically but also in their personal and professional lives.

By investing in these underprivileged students, we are investing in a brighter and more inclusive future. We firmly believe that education is a powerful catalyst for social change and economic empowerment. Through our scholarship programs, we aim to break the cycle of poverty and create a more equitable society.

Join us in our mission to transform lives through education. Together, we can make a significant difference and provide deserving students with the opportunities they deserve. Your support can help pave the way for a generation of individuals who will shape our world for the better.

Health Funds

We are committed to supporting individuals who require medical aid by providing them with essential medicines free of cost and financial assistance to ensure they receive the proper care they need. In our efforts to make healthcare accessible to all, we have also donated an ambulance to provide free ambulance services.

Our primary objective is to alleviate the burden faced by those in need of medical assistance. We understand that accessing medicines and receiving appropriate care can be financially challenging for many individuals and families. Therefore, we strive to bridge this gap by supplying necessary medicines without any cost, ensuring that no one is deprived of essential treatments due to financial constraints.

Additionally, we offer financial aid to individuals who require specialized treatments, surgeries, or medical procedures that may incur substantial expenses. Our aim is to enable these individuals to access quality healthcare services without worrying about the financial burden. By providing financial support, we hope to contribute to their well-being and improve their chances of recovery and a better quality of life.

In line with our commitment to emergency medical services, we have generously donated an ambulance. This ambulance serves as a vital resource in facilitating timely transportation for patients in critical condition, ensuring they receive prompt medical attention. By offering this free ambulance service, we aim to enhance the accessibility of emergency care, particularly for those who may not have immediate access to transportation or cannot afford ambulance services.

Through our initiatives, we aspire to make a positive impact on the lives of those in need, promoting health and well-being within our community. By providing free medicines, financial aid, and a dedicated ambulance service, we strive to ensure that everyone has the opportunity to receive the medical assistance they require, irrespective of their financial circumstances.

Relief Funds

We actively participate in relief efforts during natural calamities such as floods, earthquakes, and other emergencies. Our organization is dedicated to providing support and assistance to those affected by these disasters. Through our initiatives, we strive to alleviate suffering and offer relief to communities in need.

During such challenging times, we donate various essential items to aid in the recovery process. These items include food supplies, clothing, and blankets. We recognize the immediate needs of individuals and families who have been displaced or affected by the calamity, and our goal is to provide them with basic necessities to meet their daily requirements. By offering food, clothing, and blankets, we aim to bring some comfort and relief to those enduring difficult circumstances.

Additionally, we extend our support to underprivileged senior citizens who are in need of shelter and care. We understand that during natural disasters, older adults can be particularly vulnerable and require specialized assistance. In collaboration with local authorities and partner organizations, we provide shelter facilities for senior citizens affected by these calamities. Our aim is to ensure their safety, well-being, and access to essential services during these challenging times.

Through our commitment to disaster relief and assistance, we endeavor to make a positive impact on the lives of those affected by natural calamities. By donating essential items, offering shelter, and providing support to underprivileged senior citizens, we strive to bring hope, relief, and a sense of security to communities facing adversity. Together with our dedicated volunteers and partners, we work towards rebuilding lives and fostering resilience in the face of challenging circumstances.

Child Help

We place great importance on supporting orphanages and providing assistance to children in need. Our organization is dedicated to making regular visits to various orphanages, where we not only provide meals but also extend our support to fulfill their educational needs.

During our visits, we prioritize ensuring that the children receive nutritious meals. We understand the significance of proper nutrition for their growth and development. Therefore, we collaborate with local partners to prepare and serve well-balanced meals to the children residing in orphanages. Our aim is to address their dietary requirements and contribute to their overall well-being.

In addition to providing meals, we are committed to supporting the educational needs of the children in orphanages. We believe that education plays a crucial role in empowering individuals and opening doors to a brighter future. Through our initiatives, we offer various forms of educational support, which may include providing school supplies, books, uniforms, and facilitating access to educational resources. We strive to create an environment where these children can thrive academically and realize their full potential.

Moreover, our visits to orphanages go beyond material support. We engage with the children, spend quality time with them, and provide emotional support. Our volunteers and staff members organize activities, games, and workshops to foster a positive and nurturing environment. We aim to create a sense of belonging and ensure that the children feel cared for and loved.

Through our consistent efforts, we aspire to make a meaningful difference in the lives of these children. By providing meals and support for their educational needs, we aim to contribute to their overall development and help them build a strong foundation for a better future. Our commitment extends beyond the visits as we work to establish long-term relationships with the orphanages, continuously striving to make a positive impact in the lives of these vulnerable children.

Ration Disctribution

We are dedicated to serving underprivileged families by providing them with essential ration kits on a regular basis. Our organization has been successful in distributing over 80+ ration kits each month, ensuring that these families have access to vital food supplies.

Every alternate month, we carefully assemble and distribute ration kits to these families in need. These kits contain a variety of essential food items that are necessary for their sustenance and well-being. Our goal is to help alleviate the financial burden of purchasing groceries and ensure that these families have access to nutritious meals.

By providing these ration kits, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of underprivileged families, particularly in terms of food security. We recognize the importance of consistent support and understand that access to regular meals is crucial for their overall health and development.

Through our ongoing efforts, we strive to address the immediate needs of these families and provide them with a sense of stability and support. We are committed to making a difference in their lives and ensuring that they have access to essential food supplies on a regular basis.
